# Peanut Butter Protein Cookies

## Ingredients

- 1 Ripe banana 
- 1/2 Cup smooth peanut butter
- 1 Scoop protein powder (animal-based works best) 
- Optional: 1/4 cup chocolate chips 

## Method

1. Preheat oven to 350F
2. Mash the banana and fold in the peanut butter (helps to warm it a bit) and the protein powder.
3. Scoop 6 cookies onto a sheet w/ parchment paper (helps to use a silicon spatula)
4. Cook 10-12 minutes until edges are golden and center is no longer wet
